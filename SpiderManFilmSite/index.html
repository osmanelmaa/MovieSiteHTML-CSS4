"Spider-Man" revolves around young Peter Parker, who gains superpowers after being bitten by a genetically-altered spider during a scientific experiment. Determined to combat crime, Peter decides to use these powers. Simultaneously, he must navigate personal challenges and responsibilities that impact his life. The film follows Peter as he dons a costume and fights criminals as Spider-Man while trying to maintain a normal life as a young individual.
